Our Story

Bosky Adventures and Safaris is a Kenya based tour company handling both inbound and outbound safaris for local and international adventures. We provide unique travel experiences, high quality, customized, creative, and innovative packages that allow the maximum enjoyment of the richness of our country.

  • Vison
  • To be the leading tours and travel company in Africa, by relying on a well-motivated workforce, applying appropriate technology, providing maximum satisfaction to the customer and promote Kenya as a tourist destination.
  • Mision
  • To offer unique and excellent services for responsible tourism by providing services beyond expectations, Timely and Efficiently.
  • Motto
  • To offer personalized services to all our clients. We are a complete service provider, focused and highly motivated to ensure customer satisfaction to the best of our ability. This is in line with our vision to be the most outstanding travel agency in African and Worldwide.
